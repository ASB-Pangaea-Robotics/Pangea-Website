Who We Are

We Are Pangea

The name of our team, Pangaea [(pan- jee -uh)], best represents who we are: a group of students from around the world. 335 million years ago, Pangaea was a former “supercontinent” on Earth. We are students from grades 9-12 at the American School of Bombay (ASB), representing 7 countries and speaking 7 different languages.

Our team's FTC objectives are to inspire students to learn more about STEM, foster self-confidence & leadership, and motivate outreach among our local and global communities.
