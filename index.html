Our Mission

We provide our members with vast opportunities and exposure to new and developing technologies. We serve as role models in our community through our work in robotics and STEM education. We create a positive learning environment which encourages students to explore new fields which prepare them for a technology-based future. We inspire people with the message of FIRST, with the aim to bring new teams from all across India into the competitions of FIRST.
